Learning to Fly

Starting out in RC aviation is one of the most rewarding experiences you can have. At Converse Flying Eagles, we are committed to helping beginners take flight safely and confidently.

Instruction Program

We have designated club instructors who provide free training for new members. We use "Buddy Box" systems which allow the instructor to take control instantly if needed.

Recommended Gear

Don't buy your first plane alone! We recommend starting with a high-wing trainer aircraft. Talk to us first so we can help you choose the right equipment.

How to Get Started

  1. Visit the Field: Come see us on a Saturday morning. Tell us you're interested in learning.
  2. Talk to a Member: We'll introduce you to an instructor.
  3. Try a Flight: Many times, we can let you try a "discovery flight" on a club trainer.
