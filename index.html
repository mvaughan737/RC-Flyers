Our Mission

Dedicated to promoting the building, flying, and safe enjoyment of radio-controlled aircraft. Converse Flying Eagles RC Club welcomes pilots of all skill levels and encourages fellowship, learning, and a shared passion for model aviation. Our goal is to provide a friendly, family-oriented environment where members and guests can enjoy the hobby, improve their skills, and participate in club events throughout the year.
