About the Converse Flying Eagles

The Converse Flying Eagles RC Club is a community of aviation enthusiasts dedicated to the hobby and sport of radio-controlled flight. Based at the Converse Airport in Converse, Indiana, we provide a safe and welcoming environment for pilots of all skill levels.

Our Philosophy

We believe that the best way to build a strong club is through personal connection. Unlike many modern organizations, we prefer to meet prospective members in person at our field or during club meetings before formalizing membership. This ensures a great fit for both the individual and our community.

Thinking of Joining?

  • ✅ Visit us at the flying field during weekends.
  • ✅ Attend one of our monthly club meetings.
  • ✅ Introduce yourself to our members and see our aircraft in action.

FAA Recognized Identification Area (FRIA)

Our flying field is an FAA Recognized Identification Area (FRIA). This means our members can fly their RC aircraft in compliance with federal regulations, ensuring a safe and legal environment for everyone involved.
