Find the Field

Our primary flying field is located at the Converse Airport in Converse, Indiana. It features a paved runway and expansive grass areas for all types of model aviation.

Pilot Safety Note

Converse Airport (1I8) is a shared facility. Please be mindful of full-scale aircraft and follow all AMA and club safety guidelines. Look for the Flying Eagles sign near the field entrance.
